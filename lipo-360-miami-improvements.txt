Schema 1 — SurgicalProcedure
------------------------------
Fields:
  name          : Lipo 360 Miami
  alternateName : Circumferential Liposuction, 360 Lipo, Liposculpture 360, 360-Degree Liposuction
  bodyLocation  : Abdomen, Flanks, Back, Waist
  howPerformed  : Step-by-step surgical description
  preparation   : Pre-op requirements
  followup      : Recovery timeline
  risks         : Swelling, bruising, skin irregularities
  specialty     : Plastic Surgery
  performer     : Avana Plastic Surgery

Advantages:
  - Tells Google this is a medical procedure page, increasing topical authority
  - alternateName covers all keyword variants directly in structured data
  - howPerformed, preparation, followup, and risks make the page eligible
    for rich results in medical knowledge panels
  - Reinforces E-E-A-T signals that Google prioritizes for health queries


Schema 2 — MedicalClinic + LocalBusiness
------------------------------------------
Fields:
  @type          : ["MedicalClinic", "LocalBusiness"]
  name           : Avana Plastic Surgery
  medicalSpecialty: Plastic Surgery
  telephone      : +1-305-501-6000
  priceRange     : $$
  address        : 8700 W Flagler St Suite 250, Miami FL 33174
  geo            : lat 25.7685486 / lng -80.337497

Advantages:
  - Dual @type hits both medical and local SEO simultaneously
  - geo coordinates make the clinic eligible for Google Maps rich results
    and "near me" queries
  - telephone and address reinforce NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone),
    a key local ranking factor
  - priceRange can surface in Google local pack snippets
  - Combined with FAQPage schema, signals a fully-structured authoritative
    local medical business page to Google

🔑 Key takeaway from keyword analysis:
Why STFB & Goldberg outrank Gallardo for "product liability attorney miami":
1. Proof beats claims: STFB names the defendants it beat (Ford, GM, Toyota) and the amounts ($650M, $17.3M); Gallardo's page contains no verdict, no settlement, and no review count — nothing a searcher can verify
2. 2–3× the content depth: STFB ~4,200 and Goldberg ~3,500 words vs Gallardo's ~1,590 — on a YMYL legal query, depth is a direct E-E-A-T signal
3. Goldberg owns the stars: 4.9 from 998 reviews in AggregateRating schema means Goldberg can render review stars in the SERP — Gallardo has a review badge image that Google cannot read
4. They cover the questions Gallardo skips: both answer "who can be held liable" and "how long do you have to file" (statute of limitations) — high-intent sections Gallardo has no H2 for
5. Risk reversal at the ask: every competitor says "free" at the CTA; Gallardo's converting CTA is "Speak with Our Attorneys" and never repeats the No-Win-No-Fee promise sitting up in the header
What Gallardo must add to dominate "product liability attorney miami" (pos 9.87 → target top 3):
1. Fix the meta description CTA: the page ranks 9.87 with 243 impressions and zero clicks — the snippet is the bottleneck, not the ranking. Rewrite to: "Injured by a defective product in Miami? No win, no fee. Free 24/7 consultation with a product liability attorney — call (305) 261-7000."
2. Fix the rating, then add FAQPage schema: Gallardo already renders stars (4.2 from the homepage's AggregateRating) — but 4.2 sits right under Goldberg's 4.9 in the same SERP, so the stars are currently losing the click. Raising the review average is a business fix, not an SEO one; the SEO win is FAQPage schema, which no competitor has — an uncontested rich-result slot on a page whose H2s are already phrased as questions
3. Delete or fix priceRange: "$1500-$10000": a contingency firm advertising a price range in schema contradicts "We Only Get Paid If You Win" and undermines the strongest offer on the page
4. Put proof on the page: add verdict/settlement figures, the Google review count and rating as crawlable text, years in practice, and an attorney bio — this is the single largest gap vs STFB
5. Target the "defective product" cluster: 1,038 impressions sit in defective-product* queries where the page already ranks 6.71–7.92 — closer to page one than the primary keyword. Add a dedicated H2 block and internal anchors for "defective products attorney miami"

TRACK 2 — SEO FIXES & BUG FIX
========================================================================

1. Removed rel="nofollow" from internal links (2026-07-07)
   - SEMrush flagged 40 internal links carrying nofollow, which blocks our
     own link authority from reaching before-and-after and specials pages.
   - Fixed 4 spots across 3 shared template files (service-page photo &
     specials buttons, the before/after partial, and specials breadcrumbs).
   - Left legitimate nofollow uses (external links, form pages) untouched.

2. Refreshed stale "last modified" dates on 30 pages (2026-07-07)
   - 30 pages hardcoded a static article:modified_time, some dated
     2017–2018, advertising stale content to Google.
   - Set all 30 to 2026-07-01, leaving published_time untouched. Verified
     zero pre-2026 modified dates remain.

3. Fixed a blank Spanish page — Accessibility Statement (2026-07-07)
   - /espanol/declaracion-de-accesibilidad returned HTTP 200 but an empty
     body (blank white page).
   - Root cause: a malformed closing tag ("</</li>") broke the Razor
     render. Fixed with an encoding-safe edit that preserved the file's
     accented characters.

4. Fixed two broken URLs in sitemap.xml (2026-07-08)
   - Corrected a percent-encoded accent in a Spanish liposuction-related
     URL and a "bostox" -> "botox" typo in a before/after gallery URL.
     Both were advertising 404s to Google and wasting crawl budget.

5. Enriched page metadata for two more service pages (2026-07-07)
   - butt-implants-in-miami and buttock-reduction-miami (EN + ES):
     restored/enriched Open Graph, Twitter, article-tag and image meta
     tags, consistent with the ongoing metadata-quality pass.

    cost table, and the schema "offers" node.

  • META KEYWORDS HAVE NO RANKING VALUE. There is no meta name="keywords"; the legacy
    page-topic / page-type metas are ignored by Google. Note "botox treatment in
    miami" and "define botox injection" pull real impressions while being in NO meta,
    and "botox injection cost" is in the meta and pulls nothing. Body/headings rank.
